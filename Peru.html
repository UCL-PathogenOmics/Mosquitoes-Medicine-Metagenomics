Course Programme - Peru Fieldwork

Day 1 - Foundational Concepts
  • Welcome & Course Overview
  • The global burden of mosquito-borne diseases
  • Introduction to mosquito biology: life cycle, behaviour and genetics
  • Mosquito ecology
  • Mosquito taxonomy & key genera (Anopheles, Aedes, Culex)
  • Vector–host–pathogen triad & transmission dynamics
  • Introduction to field work – mosquito traps, Prokopack aspirators, storage and transport
Day 2 - Field Surveillance with Mosquito Collection
  • Rural trek with mosquito traps
  • Primary rainforest walk with mosquito aspirators
Day 3 - Excursion
  • Group excursion or free day
Day 4 - Mosquito Morphology & Identification
  • Mosquito taxonomy
  • Morphological identification principles
  • Mosquito taxonomy practice
  • Mosquito photography
Day 5 - Mosquito Surveillance & Wrap-Up
  • Disease surveillance & outbreak response
  • Challenges and innovation in vector surveillance – future research directions
  • Workshop: mosquito epidemiology, mapping and analysis in R
  • Wrap-up
  • Quiz / group assessment
