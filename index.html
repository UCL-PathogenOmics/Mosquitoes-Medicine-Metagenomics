Programme

Day Module Topics
Day 1 — Foundational Concepts Peru Field Module • Welcome & Course Overview
• Global burden of mosquito-borne diseases
• Introduction to mosquito biology (life cycle, behaviour, genetics)
• Mosquito ecology
• Mosquito taxonomy (Anopheles, Aedes, Culex)
• Vector–host–pathogen transmission dynamics
• Introduction to field work: traps, Prokopack aspirators, storage and transport
Day 2 — Field Surveillance Peru Field Module • Rural trek with mosquito traps OR
• Primary rainforest walk with mosquito aspirators (Prokopack)
Day 3 — Excursion Peru Field Module • Excursion day
Day 4 — Mosquito Morphology & Identification Peru Field Module • Mosquito taxonomy
• Morphological identification principles
• Taxonomy practice with preserved specimens
• Mosquito photography
Day 5 — Mosquito Surveillance Peru Field Module • Disease surveillance and outbreak response
• Innovation in vector surveillance
• Workshop: mosquito epidemiology, mapping and analysis in R
• Wrap-up
• Quiz / group assessment
Day 6 — Mosquito-borne Diseases UK Module • Welcome & overview
• Malaria I: epidemiology, transmission, clinical presentation
• Malaria II: vaccines and treatment
• Malaria III: prospects for eradication
• Dengue I: virology and epidemiology
• Dengue II: management and vaccines
• Chikungunya and Zika
Day 7 — Mosquito Diseases & Proteomics Lab UK Module Morning:
• Yellow fever
• West Nile virus
• Japanese encephalitis
• Emerging mosquito pathogens
• Case studies: malaria, dengue, Zika

Afternoon:
• Lab safety and biosecurity
• MALDI-TOF proteomics: preparation, running and interpretation
Day 8 — Molecular Lab Work UK Module • DNA/RNA extraction
• Quality control (concentration, fragment length, purity)
• Nanopore sequencing
Day 9 — Bioinformatics UK Module • Introduction to sequencing file formats and QC
• Genome assembly
• Phylogenetic reconstruction
• RNA transcriptomics
Day 10 — Bioinformatics & Vector Control UK Module Morning:
• Bioinformatics workshop: interpreting sequencing results

Afternoon:
• Vector control tools (IRS, larviciding, Wolbachia, sterile males)
• Community engagement for vector control
• Climate change and mosquito-borne diseases
• Mosquito photography review (Levon Biss)
• Course wrap-up and evaluation
