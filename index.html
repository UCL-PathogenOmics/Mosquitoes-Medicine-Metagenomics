Mosquitoes, Medicine and Metagenomics

Programme

Peru Field Module (Days 1–5)

Day 1 — Foundational Concepts
  • Welcome & Course Overview
  • The global burden of mosquito-borne diseases
  • Introduction to mosquito biology: life cycle, behaviour and genetics
  • Mosquito ecology (Beder)
  • Mosquito taxonomy & key genera (Anopheles, Aedes, Culex)
  • Vector–host–pathogen triad & transmission dynamics
  • Introduction to field work – mosquito traps, Prokopack aspirators, storage and transport
Day 2 — Field Surveillance with Mosquito Collection
  • Rural trek with mosquito traps
  • Primary rainforest walk with mosquito aspirators
Day 3 — Excursion
  • Excursion day
Day 4 — Mosquito Morphology & Identification
  • Mosquito taxonomy
  • Morphological identification principles
  • Mosquito taxonomy practice (specimens in resin)
  • Mosquito photography
Day 5 — Mosquito Surveillance & Wrap-Up
  • Disease surveillance & outbreak response
  • Challenges and innovation in vector surveillance – future research directions
  • Workshop: mosquito epidemiology, mapping and analysis in R
  • Wrap-up
  • Quiz / group assessment

UK Clinical, Laboratory and Bioinformatics Module (Days 6–10)

Day 6 — Mosquito-Borne Diseases
  • Welcome & overview
  • Malaria I: epidemiology, transmission dynamics and clinical presentation
  • Malaria II: vaccines and treatment – the old and the new
  • Malaria III: is eradication possible?
  • Dengue I: virology, epidemiology and pathophysiology
  • Dengue II: management and prevention – present and future vaccines
  • Chikungunya and Zika
Day 7 — Mosquito-Borne Diseases & Laboratory Work (Proteomics)
  • Yellow fever
  • West Nile virus
  • Japanese encephalitis
  • Emerging mosquito pathogens
  • Case studies: malaria, dengue and Zika
  • Lab safety & biosecurity
  • Laboratory session: preparing, running and interpreting MALDI
Day 8 — Laboratory Work: DNA/RNA Extraction & Sequencing
  • DNA/RNA extraction
  • Quality control (concentration, fragment length and purity)
  • Nanopore sequencing
Day 9 — Bioinformatics
  • Introduction to sequencing files and quality control
  • DNA genome assembly
  • Phylogenetic reconstruction (Xavier Didelot)
  • RNA transcriptomics
Day 10 — Bioinformatics Workshop, Surveillance & Vector Control
  • Bioinformatics workshop: interpreting sequencing results
  • Vector control tools (IRS, larviciding, Wolbachia, sterile males)
  • Community engagement for vector control
  • Climate change and mosquito-borne diseases: current trends and future projections
  • Review of mosquito photography
  • Wrap-up & course evaluation
